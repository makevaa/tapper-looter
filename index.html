Help

Gameplay

Tap enemy to defeat it and loot items. You can inspect looted items in inventory, and sell or equip them. You can't loot more items if inventory is full.

To un-equip item, tap the item icon in equipment view.

Items

Item tiers are color-coded as follows:
  • Weak
  • Normal
  • Magic
  • Rare
  • Epic
  • Legendary
  • Unique (very rare)

Higher tier items have higher maximum rolls so they can get higher stats than lower tiers. Unique items have fixed name and stats.

Item types

The player has an equipment slot for each item type.
There are 3 item types in the game:

  • Weapon
  • Amulet
  • Talisman

Weapons are divided further into types:

  • Sword
  • Axe
  • Hammer
  • Mace
  • Bow
  • Staff
  • Spear

Item locking

Tap "Lock" button in inventory to lock selected item. A small lock icon will appear on the locked item. Locked items can't be sold, and will be kept when selling the whole inventory with "Sell inv" button.

Player stats

Player stats can be viewed in the equipment view.

Magic find stat increases chance to find unique items.

Gold find increases gold gained when selling items.

Map & zones

Select map zones and click travel button to travel there. You can only travel to zones which have routes (lines) between them.

Different zones have higher hp enemies and larger drop pool. For example, the start zone can only drop items up to tier 3 (blue), and has no elite enemies (enemies with high hp and better loot).

Each zone has different unique items which they can drop.

Game save

The game state is automatically saved to the web browser's local storage. Save is loaded when re-opening the game. Save can be reset by selecting "Reset save" in the main menu.

TapperLooter is a client-side single-player tapper & item looting game made with Javascript. Designed to be played on mobile in portrait-view. Inspired by Diablo.

Graphics are from Open Game Art. Enemy images have been generated with Craiyon.
