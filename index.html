Gameplay

Tap enemy to defeat it and loot items. You can inspect looted items in inventory, and sell or equip them. You can't loot more items if inventory is full.

To un-equip item, tap the item icon in equipment view.

Item rarity

Item rarity tiering is color coded as follows:
  • Weak
  • Common
  • Uncommon
  • Rare
  • Epic
  • Legendary

Higher tier items have higher maximum rolls so they can roll higher stats than lower tiers, but not always. There are no unique named items in the game (maybe in the future).

Item types

There are 3 kinds of items in the game:
  • Weapons
  • Amulets
  • Talisman
The player has 3 equipment slots, one for each item type.
Weapons are divided further into types:
  • Sword
  • Axe
  • Hammer
  • Mace
  • Bow
  • Staff
  • Spear

Goal of the game

There is no goal in the game.

TapperLooter

TapperLooter is a client-side single-player tapper & item looting game made with Javascript. Designed to be played on mobile in portrait-view. Inspired by Diablo 2 & 3.

Graphics are from Open Game Art. Enemy images have been generated with Craiyon.
